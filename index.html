What We Do

One contractor from soil test to final grade

Most septic jobs get split between a designer who never runs a machine and an excavator who never did the math. We do both — so the system that gets built is the system that was designed.

Site Evaluation & Design

Test pits, perc testing, and full system design — gravity, pressure distribution, sand mounds, and difficult lots. Complete drawings and specs, filed with your health authority.

System Installation

Full installations with our own excavation equipment: tanks, pumps, control panels, distribution beds, and engineered mounds. Commissioned, documented, and certified.

Repairs & Replacements

Failing field? We'll tell you straight whether it's a repair or a replacement, give you a firm number, and handle the permitting either way.

Real Estate Septic Assessments

Buying or selling a rural property? Fast assessments with written reports that lenders, lawyers, and both parties can work with — before your subject removal date.
